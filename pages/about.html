About Us

At KantiPlus, we believe shopping should feel easy, personal, and close to home. We started with a simple idea — that people deserve a supermarket that truly understands their needs. One that doesn't make you choose between the warmth of a neighbourhood store and the convenience of modern online shopping. So we built both, together, under one name you can trust.

We are a mixed supermarket, which means you can walk through our doors and shop the shelves the traditional way, or browse our online store from wherever you are and have your order brought right to you. However you prefer to shop, KantiPlus is ready to meet you there. No hassle, no long waits, just a smooth and friendly experience every time.

Our shelves are stocked with a wide range of products — from everyday groceries and household essentials to general merchandise for every part of your life. We work hard to keep things fresh, fairly priced, and consistently available, because we know how frustrating it is when your go-to store lets you down.

But KantiPlus has always been about more than products. It's about people. We know many of our customers by name. We understand the rhythms of the community we serve, and we take that seriously. Every decision we make — from what we stock to how we deliver — is guided by what works best for the people around us.

As we grow, that commitment stays the same. We're constantly improving our online platform, expanding our product range, and finding new ways to make your experience more convenient. Whether you're a first-time visitor or a long-time regular, you'll always find a supermarket that's genuinely happy to have you.
